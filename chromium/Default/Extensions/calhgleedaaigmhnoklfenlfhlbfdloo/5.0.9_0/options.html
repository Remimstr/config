Sync

SiteLauncher stores your shortcuts as browser bookmarks. If you have Chrome Sync enabled then your shortcuts will be automatically kept in-sync across all your Chrome installs—no need to do anything other than enable Chrome Sync.

Suggested Usage

SiteLauncher is designed to give you quick access to your favorite sites. To make best use of it we suggest adding up to about 24 URLs you frequent often (such as email, search, social sites, etc); and use Chrome's Bookmark Manager to keep record of less frequented pages.

Launcher keyboard shortcuts

Launcher Hotkey
Ctrl + M Opens Launcher. If you wish to change the hotkey to another key combination please see instructions
Key bindings — these work when Launcher panel is showing
A-z and 0-9 Open site corresponding to key (if key shortcuts on)
Arrow keys or [WASD] Change selection. [WASD] keys only work if key shortcuts are off.
Enter or Space or E Open selected shortcut. E key only works if key shortcuts are off.
